USAID ECONOMIC COURTS ACTIVITY

In support of Egypt’s efforts to enhance the rule-of-law, further judicial reform, and enable an improved enabling environment to grow business and attract investors, the United States Agency for International Development (USAID) launched the Economic Courts Activity (ECA), a five-year project, implemented by Tetra Tech DPK.

Know More

OUR GOALS

Provide the ECs with a modern Case Management System and raising the quality of services in the Economic Courts to be consistent with international standards.
Create an informational environment for judiciary and legal communities and an identity within the EC system.
Advance human capacity and institutional development of the ECs
