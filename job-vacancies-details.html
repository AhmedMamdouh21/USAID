SENIOR DIGITAL HEALTH ADVISOR

Summary

You are encouraged to read the entire announcement before you submit your application package. Your application may not receive full consideration if you do not follow the instructions as outlined.

This position is located in the Washington, DC Commuting Area in the Bureau for Management (M), Chief Information Officer, at the U.S. Agency for International Development (USAID).

Learn more about this agency

Duties

Coordinates the development and improvement of operational processes, including; Waterfall, Agile, and hybrid solution development life cycle approaches, project management, IT governance, IT acquisitions, Information Assurance activities.

Conducts the planning, development, measurement, analysis, and improvement of IT processes and procedures by leveraging approaches based on industry best practices, and maintains the M/CIO Process Asset Library where the process assets are published.

Conducts IT Process capability and maturity appraisals, and IT process assessments, leveraging industry best practices and standards, to determine the performance and conformance of IT processes and work products.

Conducts independent project and process audits, and other verification and validation activities, to ensure that solutions meet specified requirements and quality standards; ensure projects and processes adhere to M/CIO standards.

Provides oral presentations, with supporting presentation materials and reports, to M/CIO Managers, Process Owners, Project Managers, and other Project and Process Stakeholders, to communicate the findings, corrective actions.
